# Background

`hea_demo` models high-entropy alloy composition optimization from the tutorial repository file `examples/HEA/data/oracle_data.xlsx`.
The task follows the HEA case study in *Efficient and Principled Scientific Discovery through Bayesian Optimization: A Tutorial*.

The benchmark exposes a transform-aware mock oracle rather than a real materials workflow.
Its purpose is smoke-level benchmark integration, not full scientific reproduction.
